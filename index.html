Services

Take a look at the array of services we provide and decide which one is right for your business. If you don't see what you're looking for contact us and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.

IoT

The "Internet of Things" is bigger than ever. There are projected to be more than 25 billion devices that are IoT enabled in 2018. Let us help you devise and execute a plan to integrate IoT devices into your organization.

Cloud migration

We can help you take your existing infrastructure and migrate to the further enhance productivity. We'll be able to take any current desktop based software you have and migrate it to the cloud, where it should be.

Data analytics

Analyze customer patterns, differentiate between what’s important and what not, develop plans, and learn more about your business with Feeney Technologies data analytics offerings.

Desktop application development

We can develop any desktop software that you need. Whether it’s a custom productivity tool, product for customers, something for personal use, it doesn't matter the case if you can think of it we can make it.

Mobile application development

Creating an app for your customers? Developing a game and need some assistance? We can do all of the above with mobile-specific developers that work with iOS and Android we are sure to be able to create anything you need.

Cloud application development

Develop a custom application that fits your needs. Whatever the case, we have the ability to create it. From inital design to final implementation we cove the entire range of the services needed to build a successful cloud application.

Web design

We design websites from the ground up. Whether you have a clue what you want it to look like or not we can design the website of your dreams. Stand out from the WordPress crowd with a custom website. Let us help you design your new website today.

Web development

From the design all the way to the development we'll create your custom website. Attract more customers to a website that looks great and is easy to interact with. No matter the use we can build it.

UX/UI design
